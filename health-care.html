Healthcare

Up to recently, the Health care software is not approachable to small to medium clinics/ hospitals as the software in health care sectors are either expensive or required heavy maintenance charges result in the nonadaptation of software to smaller hospitals. But thanks to ERPNext as they have a dedicated healthcare module to cater to customers in health care sectors. With ERPNext you will be able to manage whole hospital operations either it’s a small clinic or a big hospital. Hospital administrators are able to manage Outpatient records and in-patient records, scheduling practitioner appointments, retrieving patient history, laboratory procedures, managing a pharmacy, etc. resulting in better customer satisfaction or patients don’t need to carry the papers to each department like clinic, lab, consultation, etc and can be retrieved in a click.
